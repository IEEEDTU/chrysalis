Electrozard
In a world filled with circuits, running on wires, transistors, diodes, ICs, etc., it becomes difficult to separate oneself from the everyday standard thoughts of how all these are used to form the complex systems surrounding us.
If you think, you can dare to let go of the so called STANDARDS and work out something new, something better. If you think you can bend the very basic concepts of working of modern day technology into something totally different. If you think that you can think beyond the boundaries and make something never thought before, then this is the event for you.
Presenting, Electrozard, a circuit implementation challenge that will test you to the limits, that will not only require you to push yourself but also take your thinking to the next level.


Evevt Details
PRELIMS:
The prelims would consist of a paper based timed test which can be taken in teams of maximum 3 members. Based on the scores of teams in this round, teams would be shortlisted for the final round.
Slots: 3
Date: 12th April 2017, Wednesday
Time: 4:30 pm to 6:00 pm
Venue: SPS-14
FINALS:
The top teams selected from each slot in the Preliminary Round will qualify to the Final Round. Get ready for some serious Brainstorming.
Date: 13th April 2015, Thursday
Time: 4:30 pm to 6:30 pm
Venue: Edusat Hall
